Victoria Perizes

Software Developer. Biomedical Engineer

Hi my name is Victoria. I am a multidisciplinary engineer with nearly a decade of experience working at the intersection of software development, product management, and design. For over 5 years I worked at a startup where I developed medical video games and interactive experiences with the goal of democratizing medical training. My work spans multiple apps in several medical specialties and has even expanded into space medicine.
In 2021 I decided to pursue my deep interest in software development and teach myself C# to make my own games. In 2023 I decided to completely invest in myself and take a break from work so I can focus on learning full time (and indulge in some much needed travel)!
